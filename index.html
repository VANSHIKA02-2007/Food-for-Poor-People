Food Wastage

In today's scenario world's one of the major problem is Food Wastage. If we go in a party there we will see a lot of food to eat but do you think that the whole food is eaten? If you think so, then you're wrong as the whole food is never eaten because when the party is over and we return to our homes, there is a lot of food left in the hotel and that food is thrown in the dustbins and on the roads and is wasted. Do, you know 1.3 billion tonnes of food is wasted every day and there are some people who sleep with a full stomach and on the other hand there are some people who can't afford even 1 meal in a day, so they search the food on roads and in the dustbins and eat that which is very unhealthy. If they are not able to find the food on the roads or in the dustbins then they have to sleep with an empty stomach. So, food is very important and food wastage should be stopped.

Now, to stop food wastage, I will make an app in which the ashrams, colonies and the NGOs made for poor people can connect with their nearest hotels/restaurants and collect the food that is left in the party. The hotels can regularly send the information in the app such as the quantity of food left and the name of the items which are left and their address and then the ashram people can see the information and they can go and collect the food from there and for going there I will give a map in the app so that they can reach there easily by following the map, and if they have to ask something regarding the food, the hotel people can also share their contact number along with the information.To see the nearest hotels, the ashrams can turn on their location. After turning on the location they will see the nearest hotels to them, then they will get all the information given by the hotels nearest to them and not only the ashrams can go and collect the food but also the hotel staff can go to give the food in the ashram. So, the hotels can also turn on their location in the app to see the nearest ashrams to them and the food required there and their contact number. On opening the app, it will ask you for the information required such as contact number, address and whether you want to see the ashrams or the hotels near to you. If any one is having some extra food in their houses they can also use the app to give the food to the ashrams but for that they have to go to the ashrams and give the food as houses van't be detected in the map.
